1. Select Pattern and Target: Choose a pattern type and specify your target.
2. Start Writing: Click the "Play" button and begin writing your word in the text field.
3. Complete a Word: Press Enter when you finish typing a word.
4. Assess Accuracy: Click the "Grade" button to evaluate your accuracy.

For custom lists:
- Input Your Own List: If you want to use your own list, select the appropriate pattern and click "Save" after entering your input.
- Navigate Back: Press the "UP" key to go back to the last word you entered in the writing pattern.

Wishing you the best of luck in your endeavors!
